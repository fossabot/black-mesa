    What is Black Mesa?

    Black Mesa is an Aperture/*boat alternative for server owners who love configuration without compromising performance .

    Who's behind it?

    Black Mesa was developed out of necessity by Tyler and Lewis from the Troll Discord server as Aperture was an unreliable bot at the time. Flashy (also from Troll) joined shortly after as a web developer to work on the web dashboard.
